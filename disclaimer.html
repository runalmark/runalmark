Privacy Policy:

At Runal Developers, we respect our valued customer's privacy and ensure not to collect any information other than the information relevant to get in touch with you. As such, we strive to take care of our customer’s right to privacy in connection with their interaction with this website. Our scope of commitment as part of the use of your information is outlined herein.

1. Collection of Personal Information

We collect your information mainly when you contact us to inquire us about our projects. This usually includes your Name, Phone number, Email ID and Interest Area. Upon the submission of information, you give Runal Developers’ consent to use your information to get in touch with you via Phone, Email, and SMS.

2. Use of Information

Any personal information of a visitor obtained through this website will be utilized by Runal Developers to contact them to give details about products and services they inquired with us via different media sources. This information will also be used for auditing, research and activities focused to improve the performance of our website.

3. Privacy of Your Information

All information provided by our customers such as their Name, Email ID, Phone No., etc. shall be considered private, and will not be disclosed or sold to anyone apart from our various projects in sole proprietorship and Joint Ventures.

4. Non-Personal Information

We also consistently collect data from the website, with the help of patterns via web logs and third-party service providers. But this data is mostly deployed to evaluate the efficiency of our websites’ content and features.

5. Opt-Out

We provide options for visitors to ‘opt-out’ of having their personal information used for certain purposes. For instance, if you don’t want to receive any marketing material or information in any form be it by way of the newsletter or promotional emails, you can choose to request us not to send advertising information from Runal Developers by the way of “Unsubscribe” in email or writing/ sending direct mail to discontinue providing information.
